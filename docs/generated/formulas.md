# Metrics & Formulas
Auto-generated from `amazonMetricsLibrary` and `formulaRegistry`.
## ROAS (Return On Ad Spend)
**Formula:** ROAS = Ad Sales / Ad Spend
**Interpretation:** ROAS > 3 indicates efficient ad spend. ROAS < 1 indicates loss-making campaigns.
## ACOS (Advertising Cost of Sales)
**Formula:** ACOS = (Ad Spend / Ad Sales) × 100
**Interpretation:** Lower ACOS is better. Target typically 15–30% for profitability.
## TACOS (Total Advertising Cost of Sales)
**Formula:** TACOS = (Ad Spend / Total Store Sales) × 100
## CPC (Cost Per Click)
**Formula:** CPC = Ad Spend / Clicks
## CTR (Click-Through Rate)
**Formula:** CTR = (Clicks / Impressions) × 100
## CVR (Conversion Rate)
**Formula:** CVR = (Orders / Clicks) × 100
## Organic Sales
**Formula:** Organic Sales = Total Store Sales − Ad Sales
## Wasted Spend
**Formula:** Wasted Spend = sum of spend where attributed sales = 0
## Contribution Margin
**Formula:** Contribution Margin % = (Ad Sales − Ad Spend) / Ad Sales × 100
